Chat Beep (cg_beep)

New in version 1.5.0!
Related cvars and commands:
  • Vchat cvars
  • Vchat Modifiers cvar (cg_vchatModifiers)
  • Player Tracker command's mute argument (nm_playerTracker mute)
  • Vchat command (nm_vchat)
SettingDescriptionTags
Setting: -1Description: Enable all options.Tags: Default Default
Setting: 0Description: Disable.
Setting: 1Description: Play beep sound for team messages.Tags: Bitflag Bitflag
Setting: 2Description: Play beep sound for private messages.Tags: Bitflag Bitflag
Setting: 4Description: Play beep sound for global messages.Tags: Bitflag Bitflag
Setting: 8Description:

Play beep sound for non-installed/disabled/muted vchats.

This bitflag works in conjunction with other bitflags; i.e., to beep for non-installed/disabled/muted team vchat, both 1 and 8 bitflags must be enabled.

Tags: Bitflag Bitflag

SettingDescriptionTags
Setting: ally=​<keyboard key>Description: Hold <keyboard key> while selecting a vchat from the Vchat menu to send it to allies only.Tags: Optional Optional
Setting: global=​<keyboard key>Description: Hold <keyboard key> while selecting a vchat from the Vchat menu to send it globally.Tags: Optional Optional
Setting: disable=​<keyboard key>Description: Hold <keyboard key> while clicking a vchat from the Vchat menu to toggle disabling/enabling it.Tags: Optional Optional
Setting: ally=ctrl global=shift disable=altDescription: Default setting.Tags: Default Default Base Base
